How We Tested

We evaluated each tool on five criteria:

  • Output Quality (30%) — Grammar, coherence, readability, and human-likeness
  • Features (25%) — Templates, integrations, SEO tools, collaboration
  • Pricing (20%) — Value for money, free tiers, scalability
  • User Experience (15%) — Interface intuitiveness, learning curve
  • Support (10%) — Documentation, customer service, community

Each tool was used to generate at least 10 pieces of content across different formats (blog posts, emails, social media, ad copy) before scoring.
