What Makes Runway Stand Out?

1. Gen-3 Alpha — The Best AI Video Model

Runway's Gen-3 Alpha model produces videos that look genuinely cinematic. In our tests:

  • Lighting & texture: Realistic shadows, reflections, and surface details
  • Prompt accuracy: Follows complex prompts like "slow motion shot of a glass shattering, cinematic lighting"

2. Video-to-Video & Style Transfer

Upload a video clip and apply any style — from claymation to cyberpunk, film noir to anime. The results are remarkably consistent across the entire clip, not just frame-by-frame.

Beyond generation, Runway offers powerful editing features:
