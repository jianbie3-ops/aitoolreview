What Makes Gemini Stand Out?

1. Deep Google Ecosystem Integration

This is Gemini's killer feature. Unlike ChatGPT or Claude, Gemini can:

  • Gmail: Read, summarize, and draft emails
  • Google Docs: Write, edit, and format documents
  • Google Maps: Plan routes, find places, get recommendations
  • YouTube: Summarize videos, answer questions about content
  • Google Search: Real-time internet access with citations

2. Best Multimodal AI

Gemini 2.5 Pro can process text, images, audio, and video simultaneously. Upload a video file and ask detailed questions — Gemini analyzes both visual and audio tracks. In our tests, this worked surprisingly well.

3. Gemini Ultra — Deep Research Mode

The Ultra tier introduces a "Deep Research" agent that browses the web, reads multiple sources, and produces comprehensive research reports. For complex questions, this is genuinely impressive — though it takes 2-5 minutes per query.
